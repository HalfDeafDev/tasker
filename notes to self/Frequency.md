# Frequency

## Frequency Types

* Day
  * Mon, Tue, Wed, etc.
* Period
  * Day, Week, Month
* Time
  * 2am

## Frequency Sets

### Every Monday, Tuesday, and Thursday at 2am (every week)

| Type   | Value    | Num |
|--------|----------|-----|
| Day    | Monday   | 0   |
| Day    | Tuesday  | 0   |
| Day    | Thursday | 0   |
| Time   | 2        | 0   |
| Period | Week     | 1   |

### 3 times per week

| Type   | Value    | Num |
|--------|----------|-----|
| Period | Week     | 3   |

### The first Monday of the month

| Type   | Value    | Num |
|--------|----------|-----|
| Day    | Monday   | 1   |
